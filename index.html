Privacy Policy for AirAPK

Last updated: 2026-06-04

1. Introduction

AirAPK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

AirAPK does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

The app operates entirely on your local network and does not transmit any data to external servers.

3. Local Network Access

AirAPK requires local network access to communicate with your Smart TV. This access is used solely for:

  • Discovering devices on your local network
  • Transferring files between your phone and TV
  • Executing debugging commands (ADB protocol)

All data remains on your local network and is never sent to our servers or third parties.

4. Device Information

The app may access basic device information (such as IP address and device model) solely for the purpose of establishing connections with your TV. This information is not stored or transmitted.

5. Data Storage

Any data generated by the app (such as connection history or file lists) is stored locally on your device only. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Third-Party Services

AirAPK does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us
